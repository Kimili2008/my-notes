Before
Heraclitus claims that all things pass to their opposites, an immportant factor in Hegal's dialectic.
Immanuel Kant publishes his Critique of pure reason, which shows the limits of human knowledge.
The works of Johann Fichte and Friedrich Schelling lay the foundations for the school of German Idealism.

After
Karl Marx writes *The German Ideology*, which uses Hegal's dialectical method.

Jean-Paul Sartre's existentialist work *Being and Nothingness* relies upon Hegel's notion of the dialectic.
