n+1:rule
for n identical hydrogen atoms, there will be n+1 peaks.
Reason:
every hydrogen will be affect by other's electron density.
Let's assume half chance at low energy state, half chance at high energy state.
(e.g. 1 H)
two peaks
(e.g. two Hs in total)
high-high 1/4
high-low 1/2
low-low 1/4
three peaks 1:2:1
