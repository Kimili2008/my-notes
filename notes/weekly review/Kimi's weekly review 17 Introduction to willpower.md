This blogpost will be updated on a daily basis. In the digital era, willpower is becoming a severe problem due to many addicting apps and social media.
With no exception, I'm also struggling to overcome these issues, such as burning the night oil, forget important meetings. If you happen to come across this blog, then I recommend you to read 
[The Willpower Instinct: How Self-Control Works, Why It Matters, and What You ... - Kelly McGonigal, PhD - Google Books](https://books.google.com/books?id=4x1aDwAAQBAJ&pg=PA1&source=gbs_toc_r&cad=2#v=onepage&q&f=false)
This book gives you practical methodologies, along with real world examples, psychological control theories and detailed timeline based on the author's experience in Harvard. Undoubtly, you will benefit from this.
